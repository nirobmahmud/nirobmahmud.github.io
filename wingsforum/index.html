WINGS FORUM 2014

THE POWER OF NETWORKS BUILDING CONNECTED GLOBAL PHILANTHROPY

is taking place in Istanbul, Turkey, March 27-29, 2014!

View our
Network

WINGS formed in 1999 in a merger of an earlier network of community foundation support organizations and the International Meeting of Associations Serving Grantmakers (IMAG), and has been a catalyst in global philanthropy ever since.

Held every four years, WINGS Forum has become the premier event in the global philanthropy community. Focused on identifying new innovations in the sector, WINGSForum has designed a peer learning experience where philanthropy leaders could share practical lessons and inspiration from their work. To this day, the event is regarded by participants as a valuable opportunity for professional development, relationship building, and knowledge sharing.
